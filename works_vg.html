    • VIRTUAL GAME is my graduation short film at the Communication University of China in 2016. It combined live action, visual effects and motion graphics. We developed the idea from real-field AR game and integrated it with a fake artificial intelligence. I was responsible for story idea, cinematography and 3D visual effects. In a word, everything is the game is virtual! Thanks for the help from our parents, professors, schoolmates and many friends.
